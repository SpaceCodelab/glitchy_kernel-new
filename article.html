About GlitchyKernel

GlitchyKernel is my little tech playground where I dive into anything and everything across computer science. Low-level systems, compilers, networks, OS internals, hardware quirks, algorithms, security oddities, performance tuning, distributed systems—you name it, I probably have a post (or a rant) about it. This blog is where I break things down, experiment, learn out loud, and occasionally stumble into interesting glitches along the way. If you’re into understanding how computers really work—from the bits on the metal to the logic in the cloud—you’ll feel right at home. Welcome to the kernel panic.
